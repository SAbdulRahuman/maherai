## Vision 2030 Overview

[Saudi Vision 2030](https://www.vision2030.gov.sa/) is the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's strategic
framework to reduce dependence on oil, diversify the economy, and develop public service sectors
including health, education, infrastructure, recreation, and tourism. Announced in April 2016,
it charts a path toward a thriving economy, an ambitious nation, and a vibrant society.

**Maher AI — QuantOps** directly contributes to multiple Vision 2030 pillars through
AI-powered financial technology innovation.

## Saudi Market Integration

### Phase 1: Foundation (Current)

- **NSE/Global markets** — Initial data ingestion and AI model development
- **Architecture** — Build observability-driven platform on cloud-native stack
- **Maher AI v1** — Expert persona with buy/sell recommendations

### Phase 2: Saudi Capital Market (Tadawul)

- **Tadawul API integration** — Real-time Saudi stock market data
- **Arabic NLP** — Maher AI responses in Arabic (العربية)
- **Saudi news sentiment** — Arabic financial news analysis
- **Regulatory compliance** — CMA (Capital Market Authority) guidelines

### Phase 3: Regional Expansion

- **GCC markets** — Dubai (DFM), Abu Dhabi (ADX), Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar
- **Islamic finance** — Shariah-compliant investment screening and guidance
- **MENA coverage** — Egyptian Exchange, Casablanca Stock Exchange

### Phase 4: Global & Advanced

- **Multi-market** — Global stocks, crypto, forex
- **Autonomous trading** — Maher AI Agent autonomous execution (with user approval)
- **Enterprise** — White-label for Saudi banks and brokerages
